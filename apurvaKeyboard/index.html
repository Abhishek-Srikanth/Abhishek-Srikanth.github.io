The Keyboard redesigned

Ever felt the pain of finding a consonant only to hunt for a vowel to combine it with ?

Apurva keyboard is a unique keyboard specifically designed to attend to the typing needs of many Asian languages whose scripts utilize characters that are a combination of English vowels and consonants. When writing in these languages, it is rather counter intuitive to find a consonant and then find a corresponding vowel to combine with.

Apurva keyboard has been specially designed to solve this extremely bothersome issue.

Optimized layout

The unique key layout is the result of extensive research on character usage frequencies and space optimizations. A few minutes is all that is needed to familiarize and master the keyboard.

A single swipe

Apurva keyboard has target a major flaw in modern day keyboards and has come up with the best way to type! Now, each character is just a measly swipe away, gone are the days where you have to find and click on 3-5 different buttons to get just one character ready.

quick switch design

With the quick-switch design, changing from one language to another is effortless. You can now enjoy the pleasure of typing in upto 4 completely different languages at the same time.

Languages targeted

Currently supports Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, Malayalam, Gujarati, Kannada & English.
A lot more languages will be added shortly!

Customizable

Great effort has been put to ensure that you get to decide on every aspect of the keyboard, no matter how trivial.
You can easily change languages, background and text colors and even the fiddle with the time it takes to detect a long press using our preference menu.

Dedicated qwerty Keyboard

No matter how awesome your language is, there comes a time when you might need to type in English. To make sure you don't struggle with this, we have recreated the ever familiar qwerty keyboard format.
