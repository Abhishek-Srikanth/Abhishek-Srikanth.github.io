The Hungry Mage



Description:

Our poor little magician is hungry for some pizza. Unfortunately, the heinous pizza he is chasing is enchanted and it disappears whenever our hungry magician gets close to it. It is up to you now to help him find and get to his pizza by tackling more than 60 levels of increasing difficulty using some of the gravity defying (literally) magic tricks our magician has up his sleeves. Get ready for a mind boggling, head twisting and painstaking adventure to unite our hungry little magician to his magical pizza!
Now available on your android playstore.

Major challenges faced

Challenges faced

  • Game mechanics development
    • Making and managing a camera object that would display the map based on the axis of gravity and character location at a given instant in the game.
    • Deciding and implementing a method to represent all the maze levels (60+) in the game with least complication and overhead possible.
  • Creating and Managing resources for the game
    • The animation clip present at the start of each game was extremely difficult to draw and put together into one final clip.
    • Designing a character sprite that would look good even as it would make implementation of character animations like walking easy.
  • Level Designing techniques
    • Implementing easy to understand tutorial levels at the start of the game.
    • Custom designing 64 unique levels with progressive difficulty and variation.
  • Game testing
    • Obtaining data from user testing and making various changes accordingly.
