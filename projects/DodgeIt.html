DodgeIt




Description:

This is a unique game where the constantly bouncing world cup football must be saved from hordes of incoming red danger balls. An android game set in various football stadiums around the world will provide you with endless hours of challenging fun along with a gratifying, but suspense ridden ending!
Now available on your android playstore.
